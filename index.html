Our values

Transparency

Sincerity is something fundamental in our project, especially when it comes to our route to take and how we will handle the development of the same, the evolution, innovation and mutation but above all to let people know what we are working on and how each step of the process is going.

Rebelliousness

Many believe that this is not a value as such, but it is an important factor when you want to go against the current in which everyone goes and show that there are not only other ways, but that they are better than the one accepted in the community as the ideal one.

Community

A community that makes itself heard, is a community that generates change and it is for them that you, the investors of this project will be the key to make the voice of the community heard in the crowd by propagating in unison our project and the reason for it.

Loyalty

We are loyal to you and we hope you are loyal to us, that's why the fact of being investors will generate passive income without even lifting a finger, because we will have a tokenomics system that will generate TBTC tokens in order to reward your support in a way equivalent to your investment.

Efficiency

In order to comply with our route of goals, the first thing we will comply with will be the tokenomics, because the obtaining of tokens will be immediate according to each transaction made so that you, who trust in this project and in its process will generate passive income as soon as you get your purchased tokens.
